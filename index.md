# 🔒 Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 13.04.2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **Timely** ("the app") handles user data.

---

## 📋 Data Collection

Timely does not collect personal data directly, but uses third-party services that may collect certain information automatically.

---

## 🔗 Third-Party Services

Timely uses the following services:

| Service | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **Google Play Billing** | Process optional one-time donations |
| **Firebase Analytics** | Understand how the app is used |
| **Firebase Crashlytics** | Detect and fix crashes |
| **Google Play Services** | Basic app functionality |

These services may collect information such as:

- 📱 Device information (model, OS version)
- 📊 App usage data (screens visited, interactions)
- 🛠 Crash logs and diagnostics
- 📍 Approximate location (based on IP)

> For more details, see [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

---

## 💳 Payments

All payments are processed securely via **Google Play**. Timely does not have access to your payment details.

> 💡 Donations are optional and do not unlock additional features.

---

## 📈 Data Usage

Collected data is used only to:

- ✅ Improve app performance and stability
- ✅ Analyze usage patterns
- ✅ Fix bugs and crashes

---

## 🤝 Data Sharing

Timely does **not** sell or share user data with third parties, except as required to operate the services listed above.

---

## 👶 Children's Privacy

Timely is not specifically targeted at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from them.
